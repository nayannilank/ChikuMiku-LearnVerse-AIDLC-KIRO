## Getting Started

### Accessing the App

1. Open your web browser and go to the ChikuMiku LearnVerse website.
2. You'll see the **Landing Page** with options to Log In or Register.
3. The app works on all modern browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge) and Android devices.

### Creating an Account

There are two types of accounts:

- **Parent Account** — For adults who manage their children's learning.
- **Learner Account** — For students (LKG to 12th Grade) who use the platform to learn.

A parent must register first, then create learner profiles for their children.

## Parent Workflows

### 1. Registering as a Parent

1. From the Landing Page, tap **Register**.
2. Fill in the registration form:
   - **Username** — 8 to 15 characters (lowercase letters, numbers, hyphens, underscores)
   - **Full Name** — 5 to 20 characters (letters and spaces only)
   - **Phone** — Exactly 10 digits
   - **Email** — A valid email address (up to 30 characters)
   - **Password** — 8 to 20 characters with at least 1 uppercase letter, 1 lowercase letter, 1 number, and 1 special symbol (!@#$%^&*)
3. Tap **Register**.
4. You'll see a success message and be redirected to the Login page within 5 seconds.

### 2. Logging In

1. On the Login screen, select your role: **Parent**.
2. Enter your username and password.
3. Tap **Login**.
4. You'll be taken to the **Parent Dashboard**.

### 3. Adding Learners

1. From your Parent Dashboard, go to **Manage Learners**.
2. Tap **Add Learner**.
3. Fill in the learner details:
   - **Learner Username** — 8 to 15 characters
   - **Name** — 5 to 20 characters
   - **Password** — Same rules as parent password
   - **Gender** — Male, Female, or Other
   - **Relationship** — Son, Daughter, or Other
   - **Grade** — LKG through Twelfth
   - **School Name** — 5 to 30 characters
   - **Subjects** — Select from 7 default subjects (Maths, Science, English, Hindi, Kannada, Computers, EVS) or add up to 5 custom subjects
4. Tap **Register Learner**.
5. You can register up to 10 learners per parent account.

### 4. Monitoring Progress

- Your Parent Dashboard shows an overview of all your learners.
- Tap on a learner to see their activity, streak count, quiz scores, and weak areas.
- You'll receive notifications about streak milestones and progress updates.

### 5. Managing Settings

1. Go to **Settings** from your dashboard.
2. Here you can:
   - Update your profile information
   - Toggle progress alert notifications
   - Toggle streak reminder notifications
   - Manage learner accounts (edit or remove)

## Learner Workflows

### 1. Logging In

1. On the Login screen, select your role: **Learner**.
2. Enter the username and password your parent created for you.
3. Tap **Login**.
4. You'll be taken to your **Learner Dashboard**.

### 2. Navigating the Dashboard

Your dashboard shows:
- **Your Streak** — How many days in a row you've been learning
- **Subjects** — All your enrolled subjects shown as colorful cards
- **Recent Activity** — What you last worked on
- **Quick Actions** — Jump back into learning right away

Tap on any subject to see your books and chapters.

### 3. Creating a Chapter (Scanning Textbook Pages)

1. From your dashboard, tap **Add Chapter** or navigate to a subject and book.
2. Select a **Subject** and a **Book** (or create a new book).
3. Enter a **Chapter Number** and **Chapter Name**.
4. Tap **Start Capturing Pages**.
5. Use your camera to photograph each textbook page:
   - Hold your phone steady over the page
   - Make sure text is clearly visible with good lighting
   - Tap the capture button for each page
   - You can capture up to 50 content pages and 20 exercise pages per chapter
6. Mark pages as either "Content" or "Exercise" pages.
7. The app will automatically extract the text from your photos using OCR.
8. Review the extracted text — you can edit it if needed.
9. Tap **Finish Chapter** to save.

### 4. Viewing Explanations

1. Open a chapter you've created.
2. Tap **Explanations**.
3. The AI generates page-by-page explanations including:
   - A clear summary of what's on each page
   - Key concepts highlighted
   - Important keywords listed
4. You can listen to the explanation read aloud.

### 5. Pronunciation Practice

1. Open a chapter and tap **Pronunciation**.
2. You'll see words and sentences from the chapter.
3. Listen to the correct pronunciation by tapping the speaker icon.
4. Tap the microphone and read the word or sentence aloud.
5. Get instant feedback on your pronunciation accuracy.

### 6. Grammar Exercises

1. Open a chapter and tap **Grammar**.
2. The AI creates grammar exercises based on the chapter's language:
   - Fill in the blanks
   - Correct the sentence
   - Identify parts of speech
   - And more!
3. Answer each question and get immediate feedback.
4. See which grammar rules you got right or need to practice.

### 7. Comprehension Q&A

1. Open a chapter and tap **Ask Questions**.
2. Type any question about the chapter content.
3. The AI reads the relevant parts of your chapter and gives you a clear, helpful answer.
4. Ask follow-up questions — the AI remembers your conversation context.
5. Each session supports multiple questions for deep understanding.

### 8. Revision Quizzes

1. Open a chapter and tap **Revision Quiz**.
2. Choose a difficulty level (Easy, Medium, or Hard).
3. Answer the quiz questions — these are generated from your chapter content.
4. Question types vary by subject:
   - **Maths**: Solve problems, fill in answers
   - **Science**: MCQ, true/false, short answer
   - **Languages**: Grammar, vocabulary, comprehension
5. See your score at the end with a breakdown of correct and incorrect answers.
6. Track your improvement over multiple attempts.

## Streak Tracking & Progress

### How Streaks Work

- Every day you do a learning activity (read a page, complete an exercise, take a quiz, or practice pronunciation), your streak increases by 1.
- If you miss **one day**, your streak is safe — just come back the next day!
- If you miss **two days in a row**, your streak resets to 0.
- Your current streak and longest streak are always visible on your dashboard.

### Qualifying Activities

Any of these count toward your daily streak:
- Reading a chapter page
- Completing a grammar exercise
- Taking a revision quiz
- Practicing pronunciation
- Answering Q&A questions

You only need to do **one activity** per day to keep your streak going.

## Tips & FAQ

### Tips for Parents

- **Check in weekly** — Review your child's dashboard to see which subjects need attention.
- **Encourage streaks** — A consistent daily habit of even 10 minutes makes a big difference.
- **Add custom subjects** — If your child studies something not in the default list, add it!

### Tips for Learners

- **Capture pages clearly** — Good lighting and a steady hand make OCR work better.
- **Use all features** — Don't just read. Practice pronunciation, do grammar exercises, and take quizzes.
- **Ask questions** — The Q&A feature is great for clearing doubts. Don't be shy!
- **Keep your streak** — Try to do at least one small activity every day.

### FAQ

**Q: How many subjects can I have?**
A: 7 default subjects are available (Maths, Science, English, Hindi, Kannada, Computers, EVS) plus up to 5 custom subjects per learner.

**Q: What happens if the internet goes out?**
A: The app works offline! Your chapter content, explanations, and exercises are saved locally. When you reconnect, everything syncs automatically.

**Q: Can I edit text after OCR scanning?**
A: Yes! After the app extracts text from your photos, you can review and edit it before saving.

**Q: How many chapters can I create?**
A: Up to 50 books per subject, 100 chapters per book, and 50 pages per chapter.

**Q: Can two parents share the same email?**
A: Yes. Email and phone don't need to be unique — only the username must be unique.

**Q: I forgot my password. What do I do?**
A: Tap "Forgot Password" on the Login screen. Enter your username, verify with the OTP sent to your email/phone, and set a new password.

**Q: Is there a mobile app?**
A: Yes! ChikuMiku LearnVerse is available as an Android app and also works in web browsers.
